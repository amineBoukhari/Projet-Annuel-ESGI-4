# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Restaurant owners and managers who use the dashboard daily for operations oversight: checking sales, stock levels, invoices, purchase orders, expenses, and team management. Context is often fast-paced — between service periods, at the end of day, or during back-office hours. Users need to find information quickly and act on it without re-learning the interface every time.

## Product Purpose

A centralized restaurant management system that replaces scattered spreadsheets and paperwork with a single source of truth for invoicing, purchase orders, stock tracking, expense management, and team administration. Success means owners spend less time on administrative tasks and more time running their restaurant.

## Brand Personality

Professional, Clean, Trustworthy.
- Professional: every interaction feels reliable and well-built.
- Clean: information is organized, not decorated.
- Trustworthy: numbers and statuses are always clear and accurate.
Voice is calm and confident — no shouting, no gimmicks, no unnecessary flourishes.

## Anti-references

- Generic Bootstrap/admin templates (DataTables, stock sidebar navs, default Bootstrap colors).
- Overly playful or cartoonish UIs that undermine credibility.
- Corporate/consulting dense interfaces with small text and PowerPoint-like grids.
- Dark-mode-only developer-tool aesthetics.

## Design Principles

1. Clarity over density — every screen should be scannable in under 3 seconds.
2. Trust through consistency — patterns repeat so users never have to relearn.
3. Respect the user's time — reduce clicks, automate defaults, surface what matters.
4. Calm confidence — no aggressive colors, no shouting CTAs, no visual noise.
5. Accessible by default — contrast, focus states, and keyboard navigation work for everyone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Default to WCAG AA for text contrast, focus indicators, and keyboard navigation. No specific formal compliance target, but good accessibility practices are non-negotiable. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations.
